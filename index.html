About Us

Youth Justice Initiative Kenya is a youth-led non-profit organization committed to promoting justice, human rights, leadership, education, peace building and community empowerment throughout Kenya.

Vision

A just, peaceful and empowered society where every young person enjoys equal opportunity, dignity and justice.

Mission

To empower youth through advocacy, leadership development, civic education, mentorship, legal awareness and sustainable community development.

Objectives

  • Promote access to justice.
  • Empower youth leadership.
  • Support education and mentorship.
  • Promote peace and human rights.
  • Partner with communities.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Transparency
  • Accountability
  • Justice
  • Respect
  • Inclusivity
  • Teamwork
