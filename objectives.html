Youth Justice Initiative Kenya Dashboard

Our Objectives

  1. Promote youth participation in governance and leadership.
  2. Advance access to justice and legal awareness.
  3. Support education and mentorship programmes.
  4. Promote entrepreneurship and digital innovation.
  5. Strengthen peacebuilding initiatives.
  6. Champion gender equality and inclusion.
  7. Promote environmental conservation.
  8. Support vulnerable and marginalized youth.
  9. Build strategic partnerships.
  10. Encourage volunteerism and community service.
